Bra Size Calculator

Finding a perfectly fitting bra is a process of trials and errors. It's normal to try on a few sizes before deciding which fits best.

Use our bra size calculator as a starting point, and exchange sizes as many times as you need with our free 100 days returns & exchange policy.

When you find your perfect size and style, your life will really change, as ours did!

Two simple steps

Stand straight by a wall, your shoulder blades touching the surface, but comfortably relaxed.

1. Underbust

Exhale and pull a measuring tape quite snug around your ribcage, just under your bust, where the breast tissue ends. This is your underbust measurement:

2. Overbust

Now, still standing, imagine you’re just about to jump in a refreshing swimming pool. The water is warm, the sun is shining… Take a deep breath…

Pull the tape around the fullest part of your breasts, making sure the tape is parallel to the ground. Breasts are a soft tissue, so this time do not pull the tape too snug. This is your overbust measurement:

Now, let’s pick a good starting point for your bra fitting at home:

You might be surprised with the size above. No worries! This is just a starting point in the brafitting process. Once you get your new bra, we will help you find out how to proceed further.

With real data from our clients, we discovered that women with the same measurements often find their best fit in different sizes. Why? Because we all have different breast shapes and our ribcages expand differently when we breathe.

If your breasts are full on the bottom, you’ll get different overbust readings with or without a bra, and that’s totally normal! For better accuracy in determining your starting size, you might take your overbust measurement while bending over 90 degrees, without a bra.
